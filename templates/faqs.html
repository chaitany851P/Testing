Frequently Asked Questions

Inner Light Advisor offers a variety of learning styles to suit different preferences:

  • Visual: Rich visuals, diagrams, and video content to help you learn through seeing.
  • Kinesthetic: Interactive activities and hands-on exercises to engage you through touch and movement.
  • Auditory (Recorded): High-quality recorded lectures and podcasts for learning through listening at your own pace.
  • Live Lectures: Real-time classes with instructors for an engaging, interactive experience.

Once enrolled in a course, you can access all content through your dashboard:

  • Visual: Find videos and infographics in the course chapters.
  • Kinesthetic: Look for downloadable worksheets or interactive quizzes in the resources section.
  • Auditory: Recorded lectures are available under each course module.
  • Live Lectures: Check the "Live Class Reminder" section for schedules and join links.
